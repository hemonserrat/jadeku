Note
Note that the 1-Wire routines may be used with any Dallas 1-W Device e.g., DS1820.

PWR_ON (RA.1, term 18) – | 4.7K | ID_DQ (RB.0, term 6) -----------------— DQ

Note that power to the DS2401 is only turned on when reading the ID. Such power savings might be important in data logging applications.
